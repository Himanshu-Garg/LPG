Discovering the world of music

Our product allows anyone anywhere to learn playing piano without the need of tutor.

We can upload new songs to it and learn how to play them by just following the glowing leds of our glove.

Also, one can set the speed of the song which he is learning to play.

Power of learning

In future, we can use gyrosensor to make the glove recognising more than 8 nodes which can increase the number of songs user is learning by a large factor.

Also, we can use vibration sensor instead of LEDs which make the glove useable even for Blind people.

Keeping it simple

One just has to wear the gloves.

Set the song from the app and decide the speed of the song according to the expertise level of the user.
